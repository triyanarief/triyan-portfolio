Values

1 - WORK WITHOUT EGO

It’s always been about making the people you collaborate with feel the most capable they can be. Prop up the people around you and everyone’s work, including your own, will benefit.

2 - BE DRIVEN

Passion for what you do is a key ingredient to ensuring the best outcome. Push yourself and you'll see more opportunities and possibilities.

3 - ADAPTABILITY

It’s rare for a project to run its course identically to another. Be flexible, open, and willing to learn so that you can take on any problem.

4 - PERSPECTIVE

I believe diverse knowledge and experiences craft a unique mindset needed for critical-thinking and empathetic design. I seek challenges and unique problems that expand my versatility.
